# Goals

## Purpose

A minimal personal website for posting occasional articles and linking to projects, websites, and apps. Clean design with good typography — not a portfolio showcase, just a simple home on the web.

## Design Constraints

- Static site — no server, no database, no CMS
- Minimal design with warm editorial aesthetic
- i18n-ready structure (English default, extensible to other languages)
- Plain CSS, no frameworks — typography and whitespace do the heavy lifting
- Content authored in Markdown

## Non-Goals

- Not a blog platform with comments, search, or RSS (yet)
